 Phishing Case Study

Phishing is a type of cybercrime where attackers trick users into sharing personal information like passwords or bank details.

A student receives an email claiming to be from their bank asking to update account details. The email contains a fake link. When the student enters login details, the attacker gains access and steals money.

Students and young users are common targets because they may not verify sources carefully.

The consequences include financial loss, identity theft, and data misuse.

Phishing usually happens through emails, fake websites, or messages.
